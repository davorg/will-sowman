About Will

Will Sowman is an author based in south London. He lives with his wife and their two spirited cats, Monty and Python. A passionate writer with a keen interest in technology and its intersection with our daily lives, Will has always been fascinated by the vast potential of artificial intelligence and its transformative power.

Will's journey began in the corporate world, where he worked for over a decade before embarking on a freelance career. His experiences, both as a corporate employee and a freelancer, have given him unique insights into the challenges and rewards of stepping off the traditional career path. These insights serve as the foundation of his book, From Commuting to Freedom: How to Build a Fulfilling Career on Your Own Terms.

His second book, Prompt Engineering for Everyone: Unleashing the Power of AI, stems from his fascination with AI and its limitless possibilities. A self-taught prompt engineer, Will believes in the democratization of technology and aims to make AI more accessible and understandable to everyone, regardless of their technical expertise.

His Latest book, Down With Work - From Cubicle to Coffee Shop: Redefining Work in the Modern Age is a guidebook for moving away from the corporate rat race and finding a more fulfilling working life.

In his free time, Will enjoys exploring the vibrant food scene in south London, practicing yoga, and cultivating his home garden. An advocate for work-life balance, he believes that a fulfilling career should not come at the expense of personal wellbeing and happiness.
